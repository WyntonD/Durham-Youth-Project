About the Durham Youth Project

The Durham Youth Project was founded by Duchess Tabb. It was established in 2020 to empower elementary school, middle school, and high school students through educstion and social empowerment. Elementary school students in our organization will be tutored virtually in Reading, Math, and Science. Upcoming high school graduates can apply for one of our four scholarships. We are the future.
